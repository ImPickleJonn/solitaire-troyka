Privacy Policy — Solitaire Troyka

Last updated: 2026-05-20

Solitaire Troyka is a Telegram Mini App. We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

What we collect

  • Your Telegram user id, username, first name, and language code, as provided by Telegram WebApp initData.
  • Game progress: level number, stars, coins, streak counters, settings (sound, language, theme).
  • Anonymized gameplay events (level start, level complete, booster used, IAP attempted) for analytics, via Mixpanel.
  • Purchase records: Telegram Stars transactions are stored idempotently for delivery and support.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your phone number, email, address, or contacts.
  • We do not show third-party ads.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

Storage

Progress is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and mirrored to our server keyed only on your Telegram user id. Payments are processed by Telegram; we store only Telegram’s payment receipt id for support purposes.

Contact

For data deletion or questions, message @ImAPickle on Telegram.
